Privacy Policy for Sakubun-kun SP

Sakubun-kun SP uses your Google account only to authenticate your identity and access Google Sheets and Gmail services necessary for providing English sentence reordering exercises and sending results to instructors.

  • No personal information is stored or shared by this application.
  • All access to user data is done securely via Google's APIs with the user's consent.
  • Students’ email addresses are used only to send their answers to their assigned teachers. They are not stored or reused elsewhere.
  • This application does not collect any sensitive personal data.

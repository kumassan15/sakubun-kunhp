Privacy Policy for Sakubun-kun Applications

Last updated: 2025-07-01

This privacy policy applies to the following applications:

  • Sakubun-kun SP (Drag & Drop Type): The core application that provides English sentence reordering exercises and sends results via Gmail.
  • Sakubun-kun EX (Typing Type): The core application that provides English sentence reordering exercises and sends results via Gmail.
  • Sakubun-kun Mondai Sakusei (Setting Questions for the Sakubun-kun App): A question-setting tool that creates and manages Google Sheets for use with Sakubun-kun.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly, in compliance with Google's API Services User Data Policy.

1. What Data We Access and How We Use It

a. Sakubun-kun SP/EX

  • Your Google account is used for authentication only.
  • Google Sheets is used to store and manage question/answer data.
  • Gmail is used to send student answers to their instructors.
  • Student email addresses are used only to send results and are not stored or reused.

b. Sakubun-kun Mondai Sakusei

  • Reads and writes Google Sheets that you explicitly open or authorize.
  • Creates and manages question files in your Google Drive.
  • Updates a linked "Question Management" file for record-keeping.
  • No access to personal profile data such as name or email.

2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any user data to third parties. All data remains within your Google account and under your full control.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data is stored in your own Google Drive.
  • No external servers or backups are used by the application.
  • You may delete your files or revoke permissions at any time.
  • To revoke access, visit your Google Account permissions .

4. Data Protection and Security

  • All access is performed securely via Google APIs and only with user consent.
  • Only the minimum necessary scopes are requested:
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive
  • No data is transmitted or stored outside of the Google ecosystem.
  • Apps are executed entirely within the secure Google Apps Script environment.

5. Your Control and Responsibility

You retain full control over which files are accessed or created. You may stop using the apps and delete related data at any time. We encourage you to review Google Drive sharing settings to ensure your privacy.
